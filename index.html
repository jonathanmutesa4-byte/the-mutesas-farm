About the Farm

The Mutesa's Farm, located in the fertile heart of 15 Miles Chibombo,Zambia a family-owned farm dedicated to growing and raising healthy, sustainable produce and livestock. Our mission is to bring naturally grown vegetables, fruits, and well-raised poultry to homes and businesses across Zambia.

With deep roots in traditional farming methods and a love for the land, we provide only the freshest cabbages, tomatoes, onions, strawberries, goats, chickens, ducks, turkeys, and more. We take pride in ethical farming, where everything is cultivated with love and care. Whether you're buying for your family or a market, The Mutesa's Farm ensures quality and trust you can taste.

Welcome to The Mutesa's Farm!

At The Mutesa's Farm, we are passionate about bringing farm-fresh produce and high-quality livestock straight from our land to your table. Whether it's crisp cabbages, juicy tomatoes, sweet strawberries, or healthy poultry and goats—we grow and raise everything with love and care.

We’re located in the green heart of 15 Miles, Chibombo, and we proudly serve families, markets, and businesses looking for nutritious, reliable, and affordable farm products.

Come and experience real Zambian farming — where tradition meets freshness.
